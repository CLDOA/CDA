ENGINEERING - Round 3
There is no seat cancellation option available during or after Round 3 for candidates with status of Accept & Upgrade or Reject & Upgrade.

The candidate has to join the allotted college in round 3. In case candidate does not join the allotted college fees paid will not be refunded besides candidate may be asked to pay the prescribed penalty. Candidates with Accept & Upgrade status will have to join the upgraded college if they are allotted a new seat in Round 3, if candidate does not get an upgrade in Round 3 then the Accept & Upgrade candidate has to join the college that they are holding from the earlier round. Candidates with Reject & Upgrade status (if allotted a seat in Round 3) will have only the option of Accept & Freeze and have to join the alloted college.Candidates who were not allotted any seat in R1 or R2 will get the option of Accept & Freeze or Reject & Exit.
